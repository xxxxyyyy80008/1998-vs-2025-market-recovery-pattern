## Visualization 1: Price Comparison

**Purpose**: Side-by-side comparison of actual S&P 500 prices

**Key Elements**:
- Left panel: 1998-99 period
- Right panel: 2025-26 period
- Calendar dates on x-axis
- Actual closing prices on y-axis

**How to Read**:
- Compare absolute price levels
- Identify similar pattern shapes
- Note crisis points and recovery trajectories

---

## Visualization 2: Normalized Comparison
**Purpose**: Direct performance comparison using indexed values

**Key Elements**:
- Both periods overlaid on single chart
- Normalized to 100 at crisis start
- Dual x-axis (1998 dates + 2025 equivalent)

**How to Read**:
- Values above 100 = gained from crisis low
- Values below 100 = still below crisis level
- Direct comparison of recovery speed

---

## Visualization 3: Dual Timeline
**Purpose**: Price comparison with separate scales

**Key Elements**:
- Dual Y-axis (1998 left, 2025 right)
- Single timeline (1998 reference)
- Performance comparison boxes

**How to Read**:
- Blue line (left axis): 1998 prices
- Orange line (right axis): 2025 prices
- Info boxes show outperformance/underperformance

## Visualization 6: Cumulative Returns
**Purpose**: Total return accumulation over time

**Key Elements**:
- Running total of gains/losses
- Break-even points marked
- Recovery trajectory

**How to Read**:
- Steeper slope = faster recovery
- Crossing zero = break-even achieved
- Final value = total period return

## Visualization 7: Moving Average Analysis
**Purpose**: Trend confirmation and support/resistance

**Key Elements**:
- Multiple timeframes (20/50/200-day)

- Price position relative to MAs

**How to Read**:
- Price above MAs = uptrend
- Price below MAs = downtrend
- MA crossovers = potential trend changes
